Our Services

Family Preservation

Helping families stay strong and connected.

Family Preservation

We work closely with families facing challenges to strengthen their bonds and improve communication. Our comprehensive approach includes family therapy, conflict resolution, parenting support, and connecting families with community resources. We believe every family has the potential to heal and thrive together.

Domestic Violence Support

Offering safety, guidance, and healing for survivors.

Domestic Violence Support

Our specialized team provides comprehensive support for domestic violence survivors, including safety planning, crisis intervention, trauma counseling, and legal advocacy. We create safe spaces for healing and help survivors rebuild their lives with dignity and strength.

Mental Health Care

Providing emotional and psychological support.

Mental Health Care

We offer comprehensive mental health services including individual therapy, group counseling, crisis intervention, and mental health assessments. Our qualified professionals help clients manage anxiety, depression, PTSD, and other mental health challenges through evidence-based therapeutic approaches.

Counselling

Specializing in Marriage, Trauma & HIV.

Specialized Counselling

Our expert counselors provide specialized support for marriage and relationship issues, trauma recovery, and HIV counseling. We offer both individual and couples therapy, helping clients navigate complex emotional challenges with professional guidance and compassionate care.

Bereavement Support

Compassionate care during times of loss.

Bereavement Support

We provide gentle, compassionate support for individuals and families grieving the loss of loved ones. Our services include grief counseling, support groups, memorial planning assistance, and helping families navigate the complex emotions and practical challenges that come with loss.

Educational Support

Assisting learners to reach their potential.

Educational Support

We help students overcome educational barriers through tutoring, study skills training, school advocacy, and connecting families with educational resources. Our support includes working with schools, addressing learning difficulties, and helping students develop the confidence to succeed academically.

Adolescent Support

Guiding teens through life's challenges.

Adolescent Support

Our teen-focused programs address the unique challenges of adolescence including identity formation, peer pressure, substance abuse prevention, and family conflicts. We provide individual counseling, group therapy, and family mediation to help teenagers develop healthy coping strategies and life skills.

Bullying Prevention & Support

Creating safe spaces for young people.

Bullying Prevention & Support

We work with schools, families, and communities to prevent bullying and support affected children. Our comprehensive approach includes victim support, bully intervention programs, parent education, teacher training, and creating anti-bullying policies that protect all children.

Employee Health & Wellness

Promoting well-being in the workplace.

Employee Health & Wellness

We partner with organizations to create healthier workplaces through employee assistance programs, stress management workshops, conflict resolution, and wellness seminars. Our services help reduce workplace stress, improve productivity, and create supportive work environments.
